Overview

Desinging a Music Player application for a client named Musik at accencture's product Design Virtual Experience Program

Problem

This client, Musik, is focused on becoming a significant player in the music app space. Our target audience for the app is young adults who use iPhones, aged 16 to 21. While the design team has completed a first draft of the music player, user research has uncovered a huge desire from its users to read and see lyrics while listening to the song. Therefore, a lyrics component could be a significant feature that adds the value Musik wants to draw other users in and take the app to the next level.

Project Reference

1. Target Audience - iPhone users aged between 16 to 21.
2. An already made degisn draft to understand the designs and Reference by the team.

My Role

Junior UX designer responsible for adding new features in the product(Music player application).

Tasks

  1. Product Design
  2. Desinging Rationale

Tools used

  • Figma - For Designing
  • Dribble - For Inspiration and Reference

Solution

I first read and analysed the whole report of user research team and reviewed the client's need and requirements then by having the team's draft design and the user audience in mind I designed the music player application on figma and gave some more features to make it stand out from the other Music applications. As it is mostly used by Young adults I gave it a lyrics feature where the listeners can see the lyrics while listening to the song and they can also make a blend playlist with their friends.They can host group listening sessions and also they can host group karaoke sessions with their friends.

Lesson Learned

  1. Understood what goes planning an UI project.
  2. Understanding client's requirements.
  3. Learned how to work with Figma.
